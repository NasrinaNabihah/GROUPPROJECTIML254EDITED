    • Frequently Asked Questions FAQ

    Q: What is Book Heaven Online?

    A: Book Heaven Online is an online bookstore that offers a wide variety of genres, including comics, novels, children's books, and religious texts. We are committed to providing quality books and excellent customer service.

    Q: How can I place an order?

    A: You can place an order through our website by clicking the WhatsApp link to proceed with your purchase within the WhatsApp application. Our staff will assist you as soon as you message that number.

    Q: What payment methods do you accept?

    A: We only accept one type of payment, which is online banking. We will provide the account number or QR code for customers to make their payment.

    Q: How long does delivery take?

    A: Delivery times vary based on your location. Typically, it takes 3-5 business days for local deliveries. We will deliver it to you using our own vehicle.

    Q: Can I return or exchange a book?

    A: Yes, we have a return and exchange policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange the book within 14 days of receiving it, provided it is in its original condition. Please refer to our return policy on the website for more details.
