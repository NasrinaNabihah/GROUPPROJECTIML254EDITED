    • About Us

    Established in March 2010, Book Heaven Online has rapidly become a beloved name among book enthusiasts across Malaysia. Starting as a small online store, it has grown into a significant player in the online book retail industry, offering a vast selection of reading materials for diverse audiences. Book Heaven Online specializes in a wide variety of genres, including comics, novels, children's books, and religious texts. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has enabled us to build a loyal customer base, making us a trusted source for readers of all ages and backgrounds. Our online store, launched in 2010, provides a convenient and accessible platform for customers to explore and purchase books from the comfort of their homes. The website is designed to offer a seamless shopping experience, with features such as user-friendly navigation, secure payment options, and fast delivery services. In 2018, we expanded our reach by launching the Book Heaven App, available on both iOS and Android platforms. The app enhances the customer experience by offering features like personalized recommendations, exclusive discounts, real-time updates on new arrivals, and a loyalty program that rewards our regular customers.

    To further improve accessibility and convenience, Book Heaven Online joined major e-commerce platforms such as Shopee and Lazada in 2020. This strategic move has allowed us to reach an even broader audience and offer our products at competitive prices. At Book Heaven Online, we believe in the power of reading to inspire, educate, and entertain. Our mission is to provide a diverse range of high-quality books that cater to the interests and needs of our customers. We are continuously evolving to meet the changing demands of the market and to provide the best possible service to our customers. Our cozy and inviting website layout ensures a pleasant browsing experience, allowing customers to find their favorite books with ease. We also offer special promotions and discounts regularly to make reading more affordable for everyone.

    Vision

    To be the leading online bookstore in Malaysia, fostering a love for reading and making a positive impact on society.

    Mission

    To provide a diverse range of quality books at affordable prices, ensuring a convenient and enjoyable shopping experience for our customers. To create a supportive and engaging environment for our employees and to contribute positively to the community.
